The Greatest Story Ever Told!

A Quick Lesson in JavaScript

You begin your quest a new arrival in the village of Blackstone. A YOUNG LAD with a large satchel of GOLD and flask of some unknown POTION looks up to you and asks:

"Hello, there! Would you like to try my potion? You won't regret it!"

Do you TAKE GOLD or DRINK POTION?

You take the GOLD, but the YOUNG LAD'S eyes turn completely black. He roars and transforms into a gigantic dragon and belches a ball of fire into the air.

You promptly wet your pants. Before you can respond or even react, he lunges down at you and bites your head off.

You are dead.

You drank the potion. A strange feeling erupts in your belly. You begin to swell to the size of a windmill, crushing everything and everyone in your path.

Once your body presses against a sharp steeple, you explode, showering the town of Blackstone with bits of yourself to the horror of absolutely no one, for they were all crushed by your girth.

You are dead.

Would you like to play again? Refresh the browser!
